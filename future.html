Future Ideas for Brawl Stars

Brawl Stars is always evolving! Here's what we imagine could be added to make the game even more exciting.

⚔️ Game Mode: “Crystal Tower”

A 5v5 mode where players fight to control a central tower that slowly generates gems. Team with most tower time wins!

🌌 Concept Brawler: “Nova”

Nova is a cosmic controller with the power to bend gravity, pulling enemies into traps and stunning them briefly.

🎁 Star Drop Rework

Introduce guaranteed Star Drops every 50 trophies earned to make free-to-play progression feel more rewarding.

📦 Feature: Map Voting

Before matches, players can vote between 3 random maps to decide which one gets played — more control, more fun!

🏆 Championship Mode for Clubs

Clubs can register for monthly tournaments and win exclusive skins and badges based on team performance.
